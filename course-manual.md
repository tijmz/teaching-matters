## Participation
This course follows the participation rules as established in the MBCS TER-B of the current academic year:

* In the case of practicals, the student must attend at least 80%. Should the student attend less than 80%, he/she must redo the practical, or the examiner of the course may have one or more supplementary assignments issued.

* In the case of study-group sessions with assignments, the student must attend at least 80% of the study-group sessions. Should the student attend less than 80%, he/she must redo the study group, or the examiner of the course may have one or more supplementary assignments issued.

* The student must attend at least 80% of all teaching activities during the entry and specialisation courses.

## Fraud and Plagiarism

The Fraud and Plagiarism policy of the UvA is followed in this course. You can find more information on this policy [here](https://student.uva.nl/en/content/az/plagiarism-and-fraud/plagiarism-and-fraud.html).

Briefly put, we consider fraud or plagiarism to be violations of the integrity of our research programme. Each individual case registered by the teacher will be brought to the attention of the Examinations Board.

To aid the detection of plagiarism, written work is always submitted to automated plagiarism checks via Canvas.
